Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit a complaint?

To submit a complaint, log in to your account, go to the "Submit Complaint" page, fill out the form, and click "Submit."

2. How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

We aim to resolve complaints within 72 hours. However, complex issues may take longer.

3. Can I track the status of my complaint?

Yes, you can track the status of your complaint on the "Dashboard" page.

4. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

5. Can I attach files to my complaint?

Yes, you can attach files (e.g., PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG) when submitting a complaint.
